You are at level: 1

Instructions

  1. Once the game is started, you will be shown a number at once. Remember the number
  2. In the next step, input the number and click submit, if the number you have entered is right, you will be shown 2 number
  3. Now remember both the numbers and input one after the other, remember to click submit after entering each number
  4. Once done, and if your inputs are correct, you will be shown further numbers.
  5. The number of numbers to be shown will depend on the level you are, level 1 would have 1 number, level 2 will have 2 numbers, level 3 will have 3 numbers shown respectively.
  6. Once you input the incorrect number, your score will be shown on screen, along with leaderboard.
  7. You can check leaderboard before starting the game, or after the game.
  8. You can also update your name, by clicking on update name
